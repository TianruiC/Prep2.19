Great Visualization Example

In the book The Truthful Art by Alberto Cairo, the author stated the five qualities to make a great visualization are truthful, functional, beautiful, insightful, and enlightening.

I chose two data visualizations about the wind map over United states from the internet to compare and analyze what makes the one greater than the another based on Cairo’s five qualities.

This data visualization was screenshot from the website Wind Map. Here are the seasons below that why I considered it is a great data visualization based on Cairo’s idea.

  • Truthful.
  • Functional.
  • Beautiful.
  • Insightful.
  • Enlightening.
